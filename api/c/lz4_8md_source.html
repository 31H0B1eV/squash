14 ### lz4 ###
15 
16 - **level** (integer, 7-14, default 7) — higher level corresponds to
17  better compression ratio but slower compression speed.
18  - **7** — The default algorithm (LZ4_compress)
19  - **8** — LZ4HC level 2
20  - **9** — LZ4HC level 4
21  - **10** — LZ4HC level 6
22  - **11** — LZ4HC level 9
23  - **12** — LZ4HC level 12
24  - **13** — LZ4HC level 14
25  - **14** — LZ4HC level 16

27 ### lz4f ###

29 - **level** (integer, 0 - 16, default 0) — higher = better
